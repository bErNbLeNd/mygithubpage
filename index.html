About

At Hungry Vegan we strive to bring your family fresh, local, hearty plant based meals.

Grab and go affordable healthy meals. By working with River Bell Market Gardens we are bringing produce from the ground to your mouth in less than 30 km. We promote simple healthy eating rooted in a local economy, a local food system, and a vibrant and resilient community.

We Offer

  • Daily meal planning and readily available grab and go items for a quick healthy family meal. Including gluten free desserts and baked goods.
  • Fresh juices and juice cleanse programs to help reset your digestion and clear out any unwanted guests that could be preventing proper absorption of nutrients in your system.
  • Adult and childrens cooking classes and demonstrations to help balance the responsibility of food preparation in the family.

Organic Vegetable box program depot for River Bell Market Gardens with fresh produce delivered once weekly for your convenience. Local organic produce from River Bell with recipes from us at Hungry Vegan to help you use your veggies to their best potential.

Our Juice Cleanses

Getting the daily allowances of fruits and vegetables can some times be difficult. The best way to fulfill those recommended daily allowances is through juicing. Incorporating Hungry Vegan Juices into your daily diet is the best way to get all the nutrients you need in aday.

The Benefits Of Juicing

  • Extremely Hydrating our cells consist mainly of water, which is essential to proper cell function, hence they daily recommendations of 8 glasses of water a day. Juicing supplies the water you need to replenish lost fluids.
  • Promotes Alkalinity vital for proper immune function. Diseases, infections, inflammation, pain, cancer all thrive in an acidic environment.
  • Cleansing/Detoxifying helps the body remove toxins. Detoxifies the system and cleanses the digestive tract/colon.
  • Promotes Clarity clears the mind and balances your mood.
  • Natural Weight Loss burn excess fat, fat stores harmful toxins.
  • Blast of Antioxidants counteracts the free radicals that can cause cellular damage, aging and susceptibility to cancers.
  • Natural Energy no crashing or burning, consistent energy.

Our juices are made with low power juicers that reach a speed of no more than 80 rpms. This means you are getting the most nutrient and enzyme rich juice we can offer. Low power juicing allows for our juices to be viable for 48 hours. You can pick up your juices for two days and store them in your own fridge until you'd like to consume them.

All of our cleanses are offered for a 1 day, 2 day, 3 day, or 5 day duration. 6 - 13 oz juices daily, designed to be consumed every 2-3 hours one hour after waking and the last to be consumed 2 hours before sleeping with as much water and herbal (non caffeinated) tea as you like. The timing of the drinks is designed to prevent any serious hunger during the cleanse. Cleanses are assessed on an individual basis so please contact us by email if you have any questions or concerns.
